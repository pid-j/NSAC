ChickenSuite

What is ChickenSuite?

ChickenSuite can refer to:
  • The company that makes a suite of products based around the ChickenSuite brand.
  • The suite of products made by the ChickenSuite company.

What has ChickenSuite made?

  • ChickenTerminal: A terminal-based operating system equipped with a filesystem and app system.
  • ChickenFS: A filesystem used in ChickenTerminal.
  • Chickenget: An app downloader/system used in ChickenTerminal.
  • Chickenget Hub: A central place to download Chickenget apps.

How can I access ChickenTerminal?

ChickenTerminal can be accessed through terminal.html in the address bar. You can also access it through the Scratch project in case you have access to HTTPS. The latter is usually the most up-to-date version of ChickenTerminal that is accessible.

How can I access the Chickenget Hub?

The Chickenget Hub can be accessed through cghub.html in the address bar. You can also access it through the Scratch project in case you have access to HTTPS. The latter is usually the most up-to-date version of the Chickenget Hub that is accessible.
